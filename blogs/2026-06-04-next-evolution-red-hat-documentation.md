Red Hat has announced improvements to its documentation experience rolling out at docs.redhat.com, with Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform 2.6 as the initial example showcasing the new format. The updated documentation system improves navigation, discoverability, and usability for technical users working with Red Hat products. This evolution reflects ongoing investment in making product documentation a first-class resource for engineers and administrators adopting Red Hat solutions.
