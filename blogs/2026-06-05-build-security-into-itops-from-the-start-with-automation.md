IT operations is a complex area where teams face demanding workloads and must complete many tasks quickly while balancing smooth operations with fast innovation and security compliance. Red Hat Ansible helps ITOps and SecOps teams collaborate by managing risk and compliance, responding quickly to incidents, protecting data, and governing access simultaneously. This post explores how integrating security into operations from the start reduces friction between teams and strengthens overall organizational security posture.
