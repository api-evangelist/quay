AI has altered the technology landscape and added more complexity for IT teams, as AI-detected vulnerabilities now emerge faster than conventional patch cycles can address them. This mismatch means traditional time-bound routines have become a liability, creating a lag time between threat discovery, containment, and remediation. Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform addresses this challenge by enabling continuous, event-driven remediation that keeps pace with AI-accelerated vulnerability discovery.
