We are moving beyond simple prompts to the next frontier of agentic AI: autonomous systems that act across the enterprise. Customers are concerned about how to adopt these agents without losing control of infrastructure or data, and agentic AI is a portfolio problem requiring organizations to avoid vendor lock-in. Red Hat's approach emphasizes open infrastructure and data control as the foundation for sustainable agentic AI adoption.
