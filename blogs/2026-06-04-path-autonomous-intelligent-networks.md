Telecommunications service providers adopting 5G standalone architectures and multivendor radio access networks must manage billions of events across hundreds of thousands of network elements every day, making manual script-based operations insufficient. The path to autonomous intelligent networks requires AI-driven operational frameworks that can self-optimize and self-heal at the speed and scale modern telco environments demand. This post explores how Red Hat's open infrastructure approach enables the transition to fully autonomous network operations.
